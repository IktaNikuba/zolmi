Got more questions about our salon booking software? Here are some answers to your most common questions.

Is there anything to install?
Nothing! Yes, you read correctly. We like to keep things simple at Belliata and that means you can stop looking for salon software free downloads and schedule appointments. Our cloud based salon spa and salon software solution means you don’t have to worry about anything, as we look after all of the upgrades for you.
Can i use my existing computer or tablet?
Yes. Our booking software, like most mobile apps, can be used on Apple and Android devices without any issues. All you need is an Internet connection and a web browser, such as Chrome or Safari. What can be more simple?
Who owns my data?
You own your customer data and our cloud-based solution allows you to back up your data automatically with Dropbox or Google Drive, so you have extra reassurance. We realize that data is now an essential element to any business.
How can i get support?
We aim to provide you with the best salon booking software support to ensure you get the maximum benefit from Belliata Salon Software. We are available 7 days a week and aim to respond to you within 4 hours. If onboarding, live chat, email and YouTube videos to guide you through.
Is my data safe?
We use SSL (look for the padlock on the address bar of your browser) for our website, which is hosted by Amazon (yes the huge store) Web Services. As we are based in Europe, we have the strongest data protection rules in the world and have to follow tight guidelines about customer data.
Can i cancel at anytime?
Yes you can downgrade and cancel our premium salon spa software plans at any time. But don’t forget you can always have access to our free salon software if you want to grow your business and manage your salon in a smart way.
